About

PUBLISHED ON WED NOV 29ND, 2017 09:35:45 -1700

I love all things Linux and am an avid open source advocate. The project I’ve had the most activity on recently is the popular Prezto zsh framework. I’m always looking for additional projects to contribute to, though I try not to stretch myself too thin. I’ve written in mang languages over the course of my time, but have a penchant for Python and Golang. I really enjoy systems and have worked with Ansible for quite some time now and utilize it for everything (including provisioning my dev machines and Raspberry Pi’s). My not-so-secret love are build systems. There’s something inherently interesting about taking a set of objects and turning them into a finalized product. I’ve worked with Make, CMake, Autotools, Scons, Ant, a little Gradle, and Jenkins. I’m interested in learning more systems and expanding up my knowledge of current systems.

I love film and video games, though I don’t have much time for them, so I keep my ear to the ground in the indie area for hidden gems. We play a lot of board games in our house, but our most recent favorite is, Century: Spice Road. It’s great for kids and adults get a bit competitive, which is thoroughly amusing. In my “free time” - if you have kids, you know the struggle - I try to bake, smoke meat, or learn something outside of my comfort zone.
